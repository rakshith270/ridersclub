Our Bikes

RE CLASSIC 350

This Classic 350 engine generates a power of 20.21 PS @ 6100 rpm and a torque of 27 Nm @ 4000 rpm. The claimed mileage of Classic 350 is 41.55 kmpl. Royal Enfield Classic 350 gets Drum brakes in the front and rear. The kerb weight of Classic 350 is 195 Kg.

RE HIMALAYAN

The Royal Enfield Himalayan is an adventure-touring motorcycle that holds a special place in the hearts of riders seeking exploration and adventure across diverse terrains. With its rugged design, versatile performance, and go-anywhere attitude, the Himalayan has gained popularity among both seasoned riders and those looking to embark on their first long-distance journeys.
